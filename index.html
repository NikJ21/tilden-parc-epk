About Tilden Parc

Tilden Parc is an Oakland-based rapper and producer known for his unique blend of hip-hop and innovative production. With a distinctive sound that captures the essence of the Bay Area, Tilden Parc has established himself as a rising force in the music industry.

His latest releases showcase his evolution as an artist, combining raw lyricism with sophisticated production techniques that push the boundaries of contemporary hip-hop.
